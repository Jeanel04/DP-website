About Daniel Pienaar

Daniel Pienaar Technical High School is a renowned educational institution that offers bilingual education to students. We pride ourselves in providing a nurturing and inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and cultural diversity.

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for success in a global society. We strive to create well-rounded individuals who are critical thinkers, effective communicators, and responsible citizens.

At Daniel Pienaar Technical High School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that integrates both Afrikaans and English languages. Our curriculum is designed to meet the national educational standards while promoting bilingual proficiency and cultural understanding.

We believe in the importance of a balanced education, and as such, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities to complement our academic program. Students can participate in sports, clubs, cultural events, community service, and more, providing opportunities for personal growth and social interaction.

Sports

  • Rugby

    We are a passionate rugby team representing our school in various tournaments and matches. Our team is dedicated to promoting sportsmanship, teamwork, and excellence both on and off the field.

  • Netball

    Daniel Pienaar Technical High School actively participates in various netball competitions and tournaments throughout the year, competing against other schools in the region. The school takes pride in the success of its netball teams in local and regional netball events.

  • Athletic

    The school's athletics program focuses on developing students' physical fitness, improving their athletic skills, and promoting teamwork and sportsmanship. Students can choose to participate in a range of track and field events, including sprints, middle-distance running, long-distance running, hurdles, high jump, long jump, triple jump, shot put, discus throw, and javelin throw.

  • Cricket

    AThe cricket program at Daniel Pienaar Technical High School aims to develop young cricketers and provide them with opportunities to showcase their skills. The school's cricket teams participate in various local and regional competitions, allowing the students to gain valuable experience and exposure.
